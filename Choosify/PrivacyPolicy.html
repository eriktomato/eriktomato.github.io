Privacy Policy for Choosify

Effective Date: 15.04.2025

1. Introduction

Choosify is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Choosify app.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data from users. Choosify does not ask for or store any personal information, such as your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

3. No Ads

Choosify does not display any advertisements within the app. We are committed to providing a smooth, ad-free experience.

4. No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade any data. As we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to share.

5. No Internal Access to Your Data

There is no internal access to any data from users. We do not store or monitor user behavior. The app runs entirely offline and does not require any network or external access.

6. Security

As Choosify does not collect or store any user data, no security measures are necessary. Your privacy is our priority.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated effective date.
